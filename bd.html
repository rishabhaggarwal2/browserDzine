browserDzine

browserDzine is a web app built for web designers and customers who wish to get their websites designed. It is inspired by Webstiles by Namanyay Goel and the idea that web designing should start in the browser itself rather than on Photoshop. Hope you like using it. It's still in its beta version so there are many more enhancements to come.
Made by Rishabh Aggarwal on Diwali.

What describes your design?

Here, Enter what adjectives describe your design. These include adjectives relating to your target audience and the ones that set the mood of your page.


Done!

Design Variables

Here, Set the properties and basic layout material like colors, fonts and other variables that go according to your set adjectives.

Colors

Suggestions

These are the color palettes that suit your choice of adjectives. If you don't see any, the library can't match your adjectives to any stored color palette. Feel free to make a custom one.

Custom

Don't like the suggestions? Just type in the swatched below the hex code of the color you like and get creative.

Fonts

Suggestions

These are the typefaces that suit your choice of adjectives. If you don't see any, the library can't match your adjectives to any stored typeface. Feel free to enter a custom one.


Custom
